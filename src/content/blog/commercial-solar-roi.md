## The Commercial Solar Opportunity in India

Indian businesses are paying among the highest commercial electricity tariffs in Asia — averaging ₹8–12 per unit depending on the state and consumption slab. For any business consuming more than 5,000 units per month, this represents a massive operational cost burden.

Solar energy has emerged as the single most effective tool for businesses to:
- Dramatically reduce electricity costs
- Achieve ESG and sustainability targets
- Benefit from accelerated depreciation (40% in Year 1)
- Generate positive PR and attract environmentally conscious clients

## The Accelerated Depreciation Advantage

One of the most powerful financial incentives for commercial solar in India is the **40% accelerated depreciation benefit** under the Income Tax Act.

**How it works:**
A business installing a ₹50 lakh solar system can claim ₹20 lakh (40%) as depreciation in the first year alone. At a 30% tax bracket, this translates to **₹6 lakh in immediate tax savings**.

This effectively reduces your net investment significantly even before calculating electricity savings.

## Choosing Between CAPEX and PPA Models

Commercial solar customers have two primary procurement options:

### CAPEX Model (Ownership)
You own the solar system outright.
- Full accelerated depreciation benefit
- Maximum long-term savings
- Asset on your balance sheet
- Best for businesses with capital availability

### PPA Model (Power Purchase Agreement)
A third-party owns and operates the system on your roof.
- Zero capital expenditure
- Fixed low tariff for 15–25 years
- No maintenance responsibility
- Best for businesses with limited capital or rented premises

## Grid Independence and Net Metering

Commercial establishments benefit significantly from net metering. During weekends and holidays when your premises are closed, your solar system continues generating electricity which gets exported to the grid. You earn credits that offset your consumption during peak business hours.

Many of our commercial clients effectively achieve a **net zero electricity bill** over a 12-month period.

## Is Solar Right for Your Business?

You're an ideal commercial solar candidate if:
- Monthly electricity bill exceeds ₹50,000
- You own or have a long-term lease on your premises

- You plan to operate from the same location for 10+ years
